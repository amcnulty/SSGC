Our History

Stars and Stripes General Contracting LLC© started operation in 2016. From the very beginning our company has worked with the understanding that real value can be added to our customer's businesses and homes.

Our core services are in the construction and landscape industries. In order for us to best achieve increasing the value of properties, we offer a full suite of services. The company was founded with the idea to offer all the necessary services to complete a variety of task to eliminate expensive sub-contracting and outsourcing labor.

Location

We are located in Johnson county, Kansas and service the greater Kansas City area. We have strong ties to our city and support local vendors and suppliers to bring affordable services while supporting the local economy.

Current home building projects are taking place in Lynn Valley Lakes just south of the greater KC area. If you are interested in building there or in other locations please contact us and provide details.

New Home Building

We offer new home building services in the Kansas City area. We strive to make affordable housing for families by keeping our costs down and eliminating labor outsourcing. We would like to hear from you to start building your dream home!
